Could cats be astronauts?

Cats are fascinating animals surrounded by mysteries. With independent and mysterious temperaments, these felines are known for their agility, elegance, and often unpredictable behavior. However, recently, a theory has been gaining traction on the internet and among conspiracy theorists: are cats astronauts?
The theory suggests that cats are not terrestrial animals but rather extraterrestrial beings sent to carry out a specific mission on our planet. Historical accounts point to the fact that cats were considered sacred beings in different cultures and even associated with divine figures. This could indicate that cats have a much more mystical origin than previously thought.

Furthermore, there are theorists who believe that felines possess telepathic abilities and can communicate with other alien species. This belief is based on accounts from people who claim to have seen cats reacting to stimuli that are imperceptible to humans or behaving unusually during astronomical events, such as the northern lights. These and other clues have been interpreted as signs that cats could be interplanetary beings.
